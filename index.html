Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to remove Microsoft Edge?

Yes, ByeEdge safely removes Edge without affecting other system components.

Can I reinstall Edge later?

Yes, you can always reinstall Edge from Microsoft's website if needed.

Will this affect my Windows updates?

No, removing Edge won't affect your system's ability to receive and install Windows updates.
